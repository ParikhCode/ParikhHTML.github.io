Origin

I was orginally born in New Jersey. However, when I was about 4 years old, my family moved to Luray, Virginia where I was raised for the rest of my life. While growing up, I had a difficult time learning English as a second langauge, considering that Gujarati (Indo-Aryan language) was my native language. By the time I was a junior in high school my family had to move to Winchester, Virginia. It was here where I received my Advanced Diploma from Millbrook High School.

Education

I am currently a student at James Madison University majoring in a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science. While at James Madison, I have completed projects related to complex Java concepts as well as learning about different aspects of a computer system. I am also involved in the Cyber Defense Club as well as Minority Programmer Association that challenge both my critical-thinking and problem-solving skills.
